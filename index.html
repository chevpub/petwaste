About Our Calgary Service

HOW DOES YOUR CALGARY SERVICE WORK? +

We visit your Calgary property on your scheduled day(s), thoroughly clean your yard, and properly dispose of all waste. You don't need to be home - we handle everything!

DO YOU SERVICE IN WINTER? +

Absolutely! We provide year-round service in Calgary, including through Alberta winters. We'll even clear snow around cleanup areas when needed.

WHAT IF I HAVE MULTIPLE DOGS? +

No problem! Our pricing accommodates multiple dogs. We recommend bi-weekly or tri-weekly service for households with 2+ dogs.

DO I NEED TO BE HOME? +

Nope! Most of our Calgary clients aren't home during service. We'll access your yard through the gate and take care of everything.

WHERE DOES THE WASTE GO? +

We dispose of all waste at approved Calgary facilities in an eco-friendly manner. Nothing is left in your garbage cans!

HOW DO I GET STARTED? +

Simply fill out our quote form or give us a call. We'll provide a free quote and schedule your first service at your convenience.

WHAT IF I'M NOT SATISFIED? +

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with our service, we'll make it right or refund your money.

DO YOU OFFER ONE-TIME CLEANINGS? +

Yes! We offer one-time deep cleans for Calgary yards. Perfect for spring cleanup or before hosting guests.
